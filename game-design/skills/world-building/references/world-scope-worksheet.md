| Long project + Medium team | Expansive |

## Part 7: Scope Validation

### Reality Check

Answer honestly:

**Can you build all listed areas to quality?**
- [ ] Yes, comfortably
- [ ] Yes, with effort
- [ ] Uncertain
- [ ] Probably not

**If uncertain or no:** Cut scope. Remove areas until answer is "Yes, comfortably."

**Do all areas serve gameplay?**
- [ ] Every area has gameplay purpose
- [ ] Some areas are purely atmospheric
- [ ] Some areas might be cut

**If areas lack purpose:** Either add purpose or cut them.
