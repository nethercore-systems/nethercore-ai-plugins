# Player Motivation Types

---

## Extended Player Types

| Type | Primary Drive | Secondary Drives | Avoid |
|------|---------------|------------------|-------|
| **Completionist** | 100% everything | Collecting, exploring | Missable content |
| **Speedrunner** | Efficiency, records | Mastery, competition | Unskippable content |
| **Story lover** | Narrative, characters | Exploring, immersion | Confusing plots |
| **Builder** | Creation, expression | Exploring, collecting | Rigid systems |
| **Socializer** | Playing with others | Competition, cooperation | Solo-only design |
| **Challenge seeker** | Difficulty, mastery | Competition, achievement | Trivial content |

## Design by Motivation

### For Achievers
- Clear goals and tracking
- Achievements and milestones
- Visible progress metrics
- Rankings and comparisons

### For Explorers
- Hidden content and secrets
- Interconnected world
- Lore and backstory
- Reward curiosity

### For Socializers
- Multiplayer features
- Sharing capabilities
- Cooperative content
- Community features

### For Competitors
- PvP options
- Leaderboards
- Time trials
- Skill expression

---

## Motivation Conflicts

| If You Design For | May Frustrate |
|-------------------|---------------|
| Completionists | Speedrunners (forced slow content) |
| Speedrunners | Story lovers (skippable content) |
| Challenge seekers | Casual players (difficulty) |
| Socializers | Solo players (required multiplayer) |

**Solution:** Optional paths. Don't force one playstyle.
