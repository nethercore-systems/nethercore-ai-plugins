# Sonic Style Definitions

Complete specifications for each sonic style.

## Orchestral

**Character:** Rich, cinematic, emotionally expressive
**Era variations:** Baroque, Classical, Romantic, Modern, Contemporary

### Instrument Tendencies

| Section | Primary | Accent | Texture |
|---------|---------|--------|---------|
| Strings | Legato lines | Tremolo, pizzicato | Sustained chords |
| Brass | Power, fanfares | Stabs, swells | Low drones |
| Woodwinds | Countermelody | Trills, runs | Pastoral color |
| Percussion | Timpani, cymbals | Snare rolls | Subtle pulse |

### Processing

- Reverb: Hall (2-4s decay)
- Dynamics: Full range (ppp to fff)
- Stereo: Wide orchestral placement
- Compression: Light, preserve dynamics

### Compositional Patterns

- Full dynamic range exploitation
- Call and response between sections
- Building crescendos
- Counterpoint and harmony

## Chiptune

**Character:** Retro, limited, nostalgic, precise
**Era variations:** 8-bit (NES/GB), 16-bit (SNES/Genesis), Modern retro

### Channel Allocation (8-bit)

| Channel | Typical Use | Waveform |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Pulse 1 | Lead melody | 12.5%, 25%, 50% |
| Pulse 2 | Harmony/bass | 25%, 50% |
| Triangle | Bass, soft lead | Triangle |
| Noise | Drums, FX | Periodic/white |

### Processing

- Reverb: None or minimal room
- Dynamics: Limited (no velocity on original hardware)
- Stereo: Mono or hard panning
- Compression: None

### Compositional Patterns

- Arpeggio for chords (limited polyphony)
- Duty cycle modulation for expression
- Echo via delayed note repeats
- Noise percussion patterns

---

## Electronic

**Character:** Synthetic, modern, precise, produced
**Era variations:** 80s analog, 90s digital, 2000s EDM, Modern

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Typical Sound |
|---------|------|---------------|
| Kick | Foundation | 808, 909, layered |
| Bass | Low-end | Sub, reese, FM |
| Lead | Melody | Supersaw, FM, mono |
| Pad | Atmosphere | Evolving, filtered |
| Arp | Motion | Sequenced, rhythmic |

### Processing

- Reverb: Plate or digital, medium
- Dynamics: Compressed, consistent
- Stereo: Wide synths, mono bass
- Sidechain: Pumping effect common

### Compositional Patterns

- 4/4 grid-based composition
- Drop/build structures
- Filter sweeps for transitions
- Layered sounds for thickness

---

## Industrial

**Character:** Harsh, mechanical, aggressive, textural
**Era variations:** Classic (80s), Modern, Crossover

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Typical Sound |
|---------|------|---------------|
| Metal | Percussion | Hits, scrapes, clangs |
| Noise | Texture | White, pink, filtered |
| Distortion | Character | Heavy, crushing |
| Synth | Melody | Aggressive, distorted |

### Processing

- Reverb: Large industrial spaces or none
- Dynamics: Compressed, loud
- Distortion: Heavy, characteristic
- Filtering: Aggressive sweeps

### Compositional Patterns

- Repetitive, hypnotic rhythms
- Atonal or chromatic melodies
- Noise as melodic element
- Mechanical, quantized feel

---

## Ambient

**Character:** Atmospheric, evolving, spacious, meditative
**Era variations:** Classic, Dark, Space, Drone

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Duration |
|---------|------|----------|
| Pad | Foundation | 10-60s evolving |
| Drone | Bass | Sustained, shifting |
| Texture | Detail | Subtle, sparse |
| Melody | Occasional | Sparse, floating |

### Processing

- Reverb: Very long (5-30s)
- Dynamics: Subtle, slow changes
- Delay: Long, modulated
- Filtering: Slow sweeps

### Compositional Patterns

- Slow evolution over time
- No clear rhythm often
- Layered textures
- Negative space important

---

## Acoustic

**Character:** Natural, warm, organic, human
**Era variations:** Folk, Classical, Modern

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Instruments |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Harmonic | Chords | Guitar, piano |
| Melodic | Lead | Voice, violin, flute |
| Rhythmic | Pulse | Light percussion |
| Bass | Foundation | Upright, acoustic |

### Processing

- Reverb: Room or small hall
- Dynamics: Natural, expressive
- Compression: Light, transparent
- EQ: Natural, minimal

### Compositional Patterns

- Breathing, human timing
- Dynamic expression
- Natural instrument ranges
- Acoustic physics respected

---

## Hybrid

**Character:** Blended, modern, cinematic, powerful
**Era variations:** Trailer, Game, Film

### Core Elements

| Layer | Content | Role |
|-------|---------|------|
| Orchestral | Strings, brass | Emotion, power |
| Electronic | Synths, drums | Energy, modern |
| Percussion | Hybrid | Drive, impact |
| FX | Risers, hits | Punctuation |

### Processing

- Reverb: Mixed (dry electronics, wet orchestra)
- Dynamics: Controlled, impactful
- Compression: Parallel, punchy
- Bus processing: Glue compression

### Compositional Patterns

- Massive builds
- Impactful drops
- Layered sections
- Epic crescendos

---

## Lo-Fi

**Character:** Degraded, warm, nostalgic, imperfect
**Era variations:** Hip-hop, Chill, Vaporwave

### Core Elements

| Element | Treatment | Character |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Drums | Vinyl crackle | Dusty, soft |
| Keys | Tape wobble | Warm, warped |
| Bass | Filtered | Muffled, round |
| Texture | Noise | Vinyl, tape, room |

### Processing

- Reverb: Small room, lo-fi verb
- Filtering: Low-pass dominant
- Saturation: Tape warmth
- Noise: Vinyl crackle, hiss

### Compositional Patterns

- Simple, repetitive loops
- Jazz-influenced harmony
- Swing/off-grid timing
- Sample-based aesthetic

---

## Dark Ambient

**Character:** Unsettling, sparse, tense, atmospheric
**Era variations:** Horror, Industrial, Ritualistic

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Character |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Drone | Foundation | Low, menacing |
| Texture | Unease | Scrapes, creaks |
| Dissonance | Tension | Clusters, microtones |
| Space | Fear | Reverb, silence |

### Processing

- Reverb: Massive, dark
- Dynamics: Sparse then sudden
- Pitch: Detuned, unstable
- Modulation: Slow, unsettling

### Compositional Patterns

- Long, slow evolution
- Sudden intrusions
- Dissonant harmonies
- Silence as element

---

## Synthwave

**Character:** 80s nostalgia, neon, driving, retro-futuristic
**Era variations:** Classic, Darksynth, Outrun

### Core Elements

| Element | Role | Character |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Bass | Foundation | Analog, pulsing |
| Lead | Melody | Saw, lush |
| Arp | Motion | Sequenced, gated |
| Drums | Drive | 80s machine |
| Pad | Atmosphere | Lush, wide |

### Processing

- Reverb: Gated, plate
- Chorus: Thick, lush
- Compression: Punchy
- Delay: Tempo-synced

### Compositional Patterns

- Driving 4/4 beats
- Arpeggiated motion
- Major key nostalgia
- Build to chorus
